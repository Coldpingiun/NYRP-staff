Staff Trainer Handbook

Note

Everything between the " " is script, so you don't copy/type it over. It is for you to say to your trainee.


Training Introduction

“Hello everyone, and welcome to your official staff training session here at NYRP. My name is [Trainer's Name], and I'll be guiding you through this training."

"Before we begin, please remain respectful, listen carefully, and do not interrupt unless asked to respond. This session will cover rules, tools, and scenarios."

"At the end, you will be evaluated to determine if you will become an Official Moderator.”


Staff Role & Expectations

“As a staff member, your responsibility is to enforce rules, assist players, and maintain fair roleplay."

"You represent the staff team, so professionalism, respect, and fairness are required at all times."

"Abuse of commands, favoritism, or unprofessional behavior will result in removal from the team.”

"The Key Expectations are:"

  • "Enforce rules fairly and consistently."
  • "Stay calm and professional."
  • "Do not abuse commands."
  • "Report issues to higher staff if unsure."

Staff Role & Expectations

“Now, let's go over the commands and tools you'll use as staff. These are powerful, and you must use them responsibly.”

"Some core commands are:"

  • "Kick → For repeated or medium rule-breaking after a warning."
  • "Ban → For serious violations such as exploiting, harassment, or repeated offenses."
  • "Teleport → For investigations or helping players, never for fun."
  • "Server Announcements → Only when necessary, e.g., event notices, emergency messages."

Scenario Training

“Now we’ll practice staff situations. I’ll give you a scenario, and you’ll tell me how you would handle it.”

  1. Scenario 1.
      2. Example: "Player crashes into roleplay scenes intentionally."
      Correct Action: Warn them → Kick if repeated.
  2. Scenario 2.
      3. Example: "Player glitches or teleports."
      Correct Action: Immediate ban → Collect evidence.
  3. Scenario 3.
      4. Example: "Player insults others."
      Correct Action: Warn → Kick if repeated → Ban if severe.
  4. Scenario 4.
      5. Example: "Player parks cars across intersections."
      Correct Action: Ask them to stop → Kick if ignored.
  5. Question.
      6. "What would you do if a player is accusing staff of abusing commands in public chat?"
      Correct Action: I would remain calm, ask the player for proof, and handle the situation privately to maintain professionalism.

Communication & Professionalism

“Always stay professional when communicating. Warnings should be polite, short, and clear. Never argue or insult players.”

"Good example:

    'Please stop blocking the road. This is your only warning.'”

"Bad example:

    'Stop being dumb and move your car!'”

Logging & Reports

“Every punishment must be logged. Always include the player’s username, the rule broken, the action you took, and evidence if available."

"Logs keep everything transparent and protect you from false accusations.”


Evaluation

At the end of training, test each trainee with scenario questions. Example:

  • “How would you handle a player exploiting?”
  • “What do you do if a player disrespects you?”
  • “What steps should you take before banning someone?”

Conclusion

“That concludes your staff training. To check your score please head over to the training-results channel to find your determined results.”

"From this point forward, you carry the responsibility of helping run the server fairly and professionally. Thank you for your time today, and welcome to the staff team!”
