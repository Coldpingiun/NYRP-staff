Promotions

This Promotion Handbook ensures fair and just promotions for every staff member within the server. If the handbook does not specifically state faster promotion eligibility for repeated excellence, the requirements will remain the same.

Minor promotions (Recognition & Trust Growth)

These are small advancements typically within the same tier or to the next entry rank.

  • Consistent Professionalism in Tickets
  • Meeting Weekly Quota (3 Weeks Consecutive)
  • Positive Community Feedback (Multiple Members)
  • Completing Assigned Trainings
  • Going Above Expectations in Duties
  • Assisting Lower-Ranked Staff With Knowledge

Major Promotions (Leadership Growth)

These are significant advancements to higher staff levels (Supervisor, Management, or Directive)

  • Serving 1 Month Without Infractions
  • Hosting Trainings & Sessions Successfully (3+)
  • Outstanding Professionalism & Maturity
  • Consistently Exceeding Quotas (4 Weeks Consecutive)
  • Positive Feedback From Leadership Team
  • Taking Initiative in Events, Projects, or Department Growth

Leadership Promotions (HR, Directive, & Executive)

Reserved for exceptional staff who demonstrate long-term dedication and leadership ability.

  • 2+ Months of Active Service Without Infractions
  • Proven Record of Hosting Events/Trainings Regularly
  • Demonstrated Conflict Resolution Skills
  • Mentorship of Lower Staff Ranks
  • Leadership Approval & Vote of Confidence

Automatic Promotions

These are guaranteed promotions when the listed conditions are met.

  • Staff Member of the Month
  • Completing Leadership Training Program
  • Outstanding Event Contribution (Approved by Directive/Exec)
  • Successful Department Leadership (if assigned)

Promotion Denials & Delays

Promotions may be denied or delayed if any of the following apply:

  • Active Infractions Within the Last 2 Weeks
  • Failure to Meet Basic Quotas
  • Negative Community Feedback
  • Poor Professionalism in Tickets or Sessions

Role Removal

Roles may be removed if promotions were gained unfairly or performance drops significantly.

  • Failure to Maintain Activity Post-Promotion
  • Abuse of Elevated Permissions
  • Multiple Reports of Leadership Abuse
  • Failure to Meet Standards of Conduct
