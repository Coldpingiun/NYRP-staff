Examples

Moderator Examples:

These are examples for moderators.


(E.g 1) Hello. You are being warned for {Reason}, if you would like to appeal your warning please head to our comms server and create a support ticket. Thank you.


(E.g 2) Hello. Your being kicked for {Reason}, please remember to follow our community guidelines/rules.

Thank you for your patience, if you would like to make a support ticket please go to our comms server. Thank you.



Administrative Examples:

Please remember these are for administrators+


(E.g 1) Hello. Please be aware that you are being banned from {Server Name} for {Reason}.

Thank you for your time/notice for our server, you may appeal in our comms server.


(E.g 1/2) Do you have any questions before you are banned? (Remember this is straight after E.g 1)



HR/SHR+ Examples:

(E.g 1) Hello. You are being fired from {Server name} due to inactivity. If you have any questions or concerns please create a ticket in our server. (IA TICKET)
